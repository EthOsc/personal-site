Journal of Pain, 2025

Leveraging Single-Cell Long-Read Sequencing in Organoid Models to Uncover Genomic Alterations in C9orf72-ALS
J. Oscherwitz, M. Asif, M. Abdelgawad, W. H. Au , A. Lakatos
bioRxiv, 2025
We utilized single-cell long read RNA-seq to investigate C9orf72 progression across aging and cell types in cortical organoid models. We developed a novel bioinformatics pipeline paired with RNA-seq, ONCITE, to analyze our data. Identified novel isoforms and hidden lncRNA networks regulating ALS-causative genes as aging with C9orf72 progresses in deep layer neurons.

  • A Neurodegeneration Tsunami Is Coming. But We're Not Helpless.

    More than 150 million worldwide will have a neurodegenerative burden by 2050. $42.5B has been spent to develop 5 drugs controlling symptoms of Alzheimer's between 1995 and 2021. However, I see hope coming from AI bringing accessibility to drug screening alongside integrating physical experiments with omics and therapeutic robotics for motor disorders as evidence the foundation exists for accessible and high-quality drug development and symptom management for neurodegeneration.
